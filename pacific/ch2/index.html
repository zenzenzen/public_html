Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Oceans. Your stay at Pacific Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt ad a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours.

  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view.
